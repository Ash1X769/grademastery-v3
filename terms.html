Eligibility and Age Requirements

GradeMastery serves students from Year 7 (age 11) upwards. We take the protection of younger users very seriously and apply the following rules:

  • Minimum age for self-registration: 13 years old, or the minimum age of digital consent in your country.
  • Under-13 users may only access the platform through a parent-created account or a school or teacher account. Under-13 users cannot self-register.
  • Users aged 13–17 require parental or guardian consent. By registering, you confirm that consent has been obtained.
  • Parents creating accounts for children under 13 provide consent on their child's behalf.
  • Schools and teachers adding students under 13 must have appropriate data processing agreements in place and must ensure parental consent mechanisms exist at the school level before adding students.

Additional protections for under-13 accounts

  • We process under-13 user data only to the minimum extent necessary to provide the educational service.
  • No marketing communications will be sent to under-13 users.
  • AI personalisation for under-13 users is limited to what is strictly necessary for the learning service — no broader profiling.
  • Under-13 data will not be used for any purpose other than delivering the learning service.
  • AI Tutor interactions for under-13 users are subject to additional content filtering.

We reserve the right to verify age and to refuse or terminate accounts that do not meet these eligibility requirements.
