Section 3

Children's Data — Special Protections

This section is especially important. GradeMastery serves students from Year 7 (age 11) upwards. We comply with the ICO Age Appropriate Design Code (Children's Code) and treat the protection of younger users as a primary responsibility.

Age and consent

  • Users under 13 cannot self-register. A parent, guardian, or school must create the account on their behalf.
  • Users aged 13–17 should have parental or guardian awareness of their use.
  • Where parent accounts are linked to student accounts, parents can view their child's progress data through the parent portal.
  • We recommend schools obtain parental consent before adding students to GradeMastery class accounts.

What we do with under-13 data

  • We process under-13 data only to the minimum extent necessary to provide the educational service.
  • No marketing communications will be sent to under-13 users.
  • AI personalisation for under-13 users is limited to what is strictly necessary for learning — no broader profiling.
  • Under-13 data is never used for any commercial purpose.
  • AI Tutor interactions for under-13 users are subject to additional content filtering.
  • Parents can request complete deletion of their child's data at any time.

What we do not do with any under-18 data

  • We do not use children's or teenagers' data for commercial profiling or targeting.
  • We do not share children's data with advertisers.
  • We do not use dark patterns or nudge techniques designed to lead children to share more personal data than they need to.
  • We do not use children's data to train AI models for any purpose beyond providing the Service.
  • We do not collect geolocation data from any user.

ICO Children's Code — our commitments

  • The best interests of the child are a primary consideration in all design decisions.
  • Data minimisation — we collect only what is necessary to provide the service.
  • No profiling of children for commercial purposes.
  • No use of children's data to target advertising.
  • Geolocation data is never collected.
  • Parental controls are available through the parent portal.
  • Children and young people can ask us to stop processing their data at any time.
  • All default privacy settings for under-18 users are set to the most privacy-protective options.
  • We conduct a Children's Code compliance review annually.

Section 7

Your Rights Under UK GDPR

Under UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018, you have the following rights:

Access Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.
Rectification Ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
Erasure Request deletion of your data — the "right to be forgotten".
Portability Receive your data in a machine-readable format to transfer elsewhere.
Restriction Ask us to limit how we process your data while a dispute is resolved.
Object Object to processing based on legitimate interests.
Withdraw consent Where processing is based on consent, withdraw it at any time — this does not affect past processing.
Complaints Lodge a complaint with the ICO if you are unhappy with our response.

To exercise any of these rights, email hello@grademastery.co.uk with the subject line "Data Subject Request". We will respond within 30 days. There is no charge for making a request.

Parents exercising rights on behalf of children

Parents and guardians may exercise these rights on behalf of their child. We may ask for reasonable proof that you are the child's parent or guardian before responding.

If you are unhappy with our response

You have the right to complain to the ICO at ico.org.uk/make-a-complaint or by calling 0303 123 1113. We would always prefer the chance to address your concern directly first.

Section 8

Cookies and Local Storage

We use browser local storage — not traditional third-party cookies — for the following purposes:

  • Session management: keeping you logged in between visits
  • Preferences: remembering settings such as dark mode and view preferences
  • Performance: storing your card progress locally so the app loads faster

We do not use advertising cookies, third-party tracking cookies, or any technology designed to follow you across other websites.

You can clear local storage at any time through your browser settings. This will log you out of the Service and reset local preferences.

Section 9

AI and Your Data

Our AI features (AI Tutor, Smart Grading AI, AI Learning Coach) are powered by Anthropic's Claude API. Here is how your data is handled when you use them:

  • Your inputs are sent to Anthropic's API for processing. Anthropic does not store your conversations for model training beyond what their standard API terms provide.
  • AI conversations are stored in our own database to maintain context within your session and to allow the Learning Coach to identify patterns in your progress over time.
  • You can request deletion of your AI conversation history at any time by emailing hello@grademastery.co.uk.
  • We do not use your AI conversations to train our own models or to share with third parties beyond those named in this policy.
  • For under-13 users, AI interactions are subject to additional content filtering and stricter data minimisation.

For full details of how Anthropic handles data, see their privacy policy at anthropic.com/privacy.

Section 10

Security

We take a layered approach to security:

  • Encryption in transit: all data is transmitted over HTTPS. We enforce HTTPS on all connections.
  • Row Level Security: our database uses Supabase Row Level Security (RLS) — each user can only access their own data, enforced at the database layer.
  • Payment security: payment processing is handled entirely by Stripe. We never see, store, or transmit your card details.
  • Access controls: access to production systems is restricted to authorised personnel only.
  • Regular reviews: we conduct periodic security reviews of our infrastructure and code.

No system is completely secure. If you believe you have found a security vulnerability, please report it responsibly to hello@grademastery.co.uk and we will investigate promptly.
